Meet Sarah

A teacher who truly cares

I'm Sarah, and I've spent over 20 years in primary education as a classroom teacher, SENDCo, Inclusion Lead, Senior Leader and Safeguarding Lead. I'm also a mother of two primary-aged children, so I understand learning from both sides: as a teacher and leader within school, and as a parent supporting children day to day.

My approach is calm, supportive and entirely tailored to your child's individual needs. I'm passionate about building long-term confidence and a genuine love of learning, not just results on a page.

Sessions take place in my home in Wickham, a nurturing and welcoming space. I also offer online sessions for families who need flexibility.

My purpose

  • Provide holistic, child-centred learning tailored to each individual
  • Foster resilience and confidence that extends beyond the classroom
  • Champion inclusive education so every child can reach their potential
  • Support families in celebrating their child's unique educational journey
